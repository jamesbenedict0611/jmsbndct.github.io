Mt. Firmament


A secluded island lies in the southeastern ocean of Huanglong, shrouded in mist that obscures its ties to the secular world. Mt. Firmament, therefore, serves as the ancestral homeland of the Jinzhou inhabitants, steeped in legend as the place where Sentinel Jué descended to guide them. Moreover, time behaves differently on Mt. Firmament, influenced by the enigmatic Temporal Mandate of Sentinel Jué, causing time to ebb and flow unpredictably. Consequently, the first people of Jinzhou emerged from this mystical mountain, forever connecting their fate to its mysterious essence.
