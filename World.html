In this chaotic landscape, human civilization grapples with the relentless trials of survival in a world that seems to shift beneath their feet. Once stable societies now find their foundations shaken, as established systems and beliefs crumble under the weight of adversity. In the face of such upheaval, society must innovate and adapt, embracing change as they confront the relentless waves of destruction and rebirth that characterize this new era. "

The imposing Mountains of Spirit run from north to south, serving as a natural divide between Inner Huanglong, which houses the capital and its allied cities, and Outer Huanglong, where the city of Jinzhou thrives.
