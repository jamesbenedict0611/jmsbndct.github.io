Whining Aix's Mire


Whining Aix's Mire is a haunting region situated in the southern part of Huanglong, characterized by its ancient landforms and crumbling ruins that tell stories of a forgotten era. The landscape is littered with remnants of destroyed buildings, creating an eerie atmosphere that disorients any who dare to explore. A massive storm envelops the area, blocking all movement and obscuring the path to its heart. This tempest is believed to be the result of a mysterious monster lurking at the center, casting a dark shadow over the mire. Adventurers are drawn to its secrets, but the dangers within may prove too great for even the most intrepid souls.
