Blackshores


Blackshores is situated on a remote archipelago in Huanglong, built upon a vast land mass of Tacetite ore that remains sealed away from the outside world by thick digital fog. As a result, this enigmatic location boasts the most advanced technology in existence, hidden from prying eyes. Furthermore, it serves as the sanctuary for the Guardians of Huanglong, who protect the secrets of this high-tech haven. The combination of isolation and innovation creates an atmosphere of both mystery and power, making Blackshores a pivotal stronghold in the realm.
