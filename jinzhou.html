Jinzhou


Known as the throat of Huanglong, Jinzhou is nestled within a vast lake, embraced by water on three sides and cradled by mountains on the fourth. This fortified city experiences a humid climate characterized by four distinct seasons and serves as a crucial stronghold against the looming threat of Tacet Discords. Founded by the Rover during their last awakening, Jinzhou is the most recent city established in Huanglong and the only one in Outer Huanglong.
